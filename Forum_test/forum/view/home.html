Chinese Development Forum Student Think Tank

The Mission

Our MISSION of this China Development Student Think Tank (Syracuse University Chapter) is to provide constructive academic reports, intellectual ideas, academic programs regarding to Chinese economic development, political reform, cultural diversity, and diplomatic strategy to students who interest in China and Sino-U.S. relations. All missions will be provided by the executive board of China Development Student Think Tank (Syracuse University Chapter) and its higher authority, China Development Student Think Tank (North America Committee) of China Development Forum, one of the most influential non-government think tank in Beijing, China.

The Goal

With our professional and intelligent projects, our members are expected to enjoy a comprehensive understanding toward China's political and economic reforms that profoundly influence not only this country but also the entire world we all live in. They are also expected to construct excellent works about their China-related topics, which may not only be the best in their classes but also get published within the think tank or in renowned academic periodicals. More importantly, the academic communications the think tank provides are also expected to construct a better mutual insight between Chinese college students American college students.

The Fun

China Development Student Think Tank is a big joyful family in which all family members study together and have fun together. As an online community, our website enable members in different universities to discuss their interested topics and make friends with each other, while it is also a place where students can participate our exclusive forums together and get inspired by the leading experts in many academic fields. Meanwhile, our offline social and cultural events provide stunning opportunities to create international horizon and strong social connection. Excellent members of the think tank, furthermore, may be offered internship or research opportunity in governmental/nongovernmental sectors in China.
